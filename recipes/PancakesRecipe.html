A Simple Pancake Recipe

Description

A pancake is a thin, flat, circular piece of cooked batter made from milk, flour, and eggs. Pancakes are often rolled up or folded and eaten hot with a sweet or savoury filling inside

The recipe can take up to 10 minutes to prepare. It is easy to cook since it takes about 20 minutes. This is the Nutrition content per pancake:

  • 61 kcal
  • 2g fat
  • 1g saturates
  • 7g carbohydrates
  • 1g sugars
  • 0g fibre
  • 3g protein
  • 0.1g salt

Ingredients

The ingredients required to make Pancakes include:

  • 100g plain flour or all purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 150ml water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 table spoon sunflower or vegetable oil plus a little extra for frying

Some optional Ingredients include:

  • 1/4 TeaSpoon Baking Powder
  • Lemon wedges to serve
  • caster sugar to serve

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar and salt into a medium bowl and whisk to mix. Set aside
  2. Mix milk and melted butter in a cup whisk to mix
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk to combine
  4. Mix well. Do not worry about a few soft lumps. Let batter sit at least 15 minutes if you have time at room temperature (or refrigerate) or start cooking right away
  5. Heat a nonstick skillet/pan over medium heat
  6. Lightly coat with butter, add 1/3 cup batter and swirl to completely cover bottom of skillet/pan
  7. Cook until underside of crepe is golden brown about 2/3 minutes
  8. Use a rubber spatula to loosen the edge of crepe and quickly flip
  9. Let the second side cook for 1 minute more
  10. Slide crepe out of skillet/non stick pan and repeat with remaining batter until finished
  11. Serve plain or stuff with your favorite condiments, toppings or drinks
